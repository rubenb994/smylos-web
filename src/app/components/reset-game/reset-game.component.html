Epeo knows he will never be able to leave the shades of society. He just wants to keep his business running and uses the parts of the potions to improve his weaker ones, making his business even more lucrative.

However, Epeo is not alone in Smylos. The wealth based system has moved more people to shady activities. A competition is growing between different groups that navigate outside the system. In a culture where sharing is un thought of, the one in power can never maintain his position at the top for long. Every retake of power, the stakes reach higher until Epeo is outrunned by his enemies. Only then he realises that his manipulative approach has only brought him short term results but nothing or no one has stayed with him until the end.

But thanks to you he can restore his smile and make up with his wife again.

Well done, you have decided to give the potion to Sisyphus and therefore to throw it into the well. Although Sisyphus might seem confused, he is dealing with the constant pressure of a social system that he aims to destroy. But from now on he will be free, there is no longer a use for the office. The potion has contaminated the drinking water and everyone now has a big smile.

His job is gone, his son won’t see him. Up until now he has been driven by his search for the potion. A silence strikes his life and he turns away from his feeling, unable to write like he did in his youth.
